## How to Generate Real Estate Leads in Mumbai

### Step 1: Segment Your Mumbai Target Market

Mumbai isn't one market. It's many:
- **Young professionals** (25-35): 1-2 BHK in Andheri, Bandra, Worli
- **Families** (35-50): 3+ BHK villas in suburbs (Thane, Navi Mumbai)
- **Investors** (45+): Commercial plots, pre-launch projects
- **NRI buyers**: Luxury properties, investment portfolios

Each segment needs different ads, messaging, and offers.

### Step 2: Run Intent-Led Campaigns

**Campaign 1: Search Ads (Google)**
- Keywords: "2BHK flats Mumbai", "luxury homes Bandra", "property for sale Mumbai"
- Budget: ₹50,000/month
- Expected leads: 80-100/month
- Cost per lead: ₹250-400

**Campaign 2: Targeting Ads (Meta)**
- Target: 25-50 age, ₹50 lakh+ annual income, property interests
- Locations: Mumbai city, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Pune
- Budget: ₹50,000/month  
- Expected leads: 120-150/month
- Cost per lead: ₹200-300

**Campaign 3: Retargeting**
- Show ads to website visitors, interested prospects
- Budget: ₹20,000/month
- Expected leads: 50-80/month
- Conversion rate: 15-20% (higher quality)

### Step 3: Send to a Proper Landing Page

Your website homepage doesn't convert.

Create project-specific landing pages:
- One for "2BHK Ready to Move"
- One for "Luxury Villas"
- One for "Commercial Plots"

Each page shows:
- Clear offer + price
- Photos/video tour
- CTA: "View Details" or "Schedule Site Visit"
- Trust signals: approvals, possession status, testimonials

Conversion rate: **8-12%** of clicks → leads

### Step 4: Automate Follow-Up

Lead comes in → 70% go cold because follow-up is slow.

Solution:
1. **Instant WhatsApp**: Auto-sends property details, virtual tour, booking link
2. **CRM automation**: Tags leads by interest level (hot, warm, cold)
3. **Sales workflow**: Sales team gets alerts for hot leads
4. **Drip campaigns**: Weekly property updates for warm leads

Result: **22% of leads convert** (vs 8% without automation)
