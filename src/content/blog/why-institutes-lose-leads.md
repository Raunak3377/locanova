Most institutes do not have a lead problem. They have a conversion process problem.

## Where leaks happen

- Leads are contacted late
- Counselors are not tracked by stage
- Follow-up happens without a timeline

## Fix the system

- Set response SLA for every new lead
- Use CRM stage tracking for each counselor
- Automate reminders and WhatsApp nurturing
- Review conversion metrics weekly

## Conversion advantage

Institutes that fix follow-up discipline usually see faster admission closures without increasing ad spend.
