## How to Generate Real Estate Leads in Bangalore

### Step 1: Segment Bangalore's Real Estate Buyers

Bangalore isn't one market:
- **IT professionals** (25-35): 1-2 BHK in Whitefield, Indiranagar, Koramangala (₹40-80L)
- **Families** (35-50): 3-4 BHK in suburbs (Sarjapur, Marathahalli) (₹80-150L)
- **Investors**: Commercial plots, pre-launch projects (₹25-100L+)
- **NRI returnees**: Luxury properties, gated communities (₹150L+)

Each segment has different triggers, budgets, and decision timelines.

### Step 2: Multi-Channel Campaign Strategy

**Campaign 1: Google Search Ads**
- Keywords: "2BHK flats Whitefield", "luxury homes Bangalore", "property for sale Bangalore"
- Monthly budget: ₹40,000
- Expected leads: 100-120
- Cost per lead: ₹250-350

**Campaign 2: Meta Lifestyle Targeting**
- Target: Age 25-50, income ₹50L+, interests in tech/lifestyle/property
- Locations: Bangalore, surrounding areas
- Monthly budget: ₹60,000
- Expected leads: 140-180
- Cost per lead: ₹200-300

**Campaign 3: LinkedIn (HNI Investors)**
- Target: Business owners, investors, 45+ age
- Monthly budget: ₹25,000
- Expected leads: 30-50
- Cost per lead: ₹300-400 (higher value)

### Step 3: Project-Specific Landing Pages

Don't send all traffic to homepage. Create pages for:
- "2BHK Ready to Move - Whitefield"
- "3BHK Luxury Villas - Sarjapur"
- "Commercial Plots - Tech Park"

Each shows:
- Project details + floor plans
- Virtual tour
- Price range + EMI calculator
- CTA: "Arrange Site Visit" or "Request Details"

Conversion: **10-15% of clicks** (vs 3% to homepage)

### Step 4: Instant WhatsApp + CRM Integration

Lead comes in →  WhatsApp auto-sends project details, virtual tour link, site visit schedule

CRM tags lead by:
- Interest level (hot/warm/cold)
- Buyer type (investor/end-user)
- Budget range
- Timeline

Sales team gets alerts for HOT leads + auto-follow-up for WARM leads.

Result: **20% conversion rate** (vs 6% without automation)
