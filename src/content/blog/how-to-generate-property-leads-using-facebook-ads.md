Real estate Facebook campaigns should optimize for qualified buyers, not cheap form submissions.

## Start with intent

- Segment buyers by budget, location, and property type
- Align ad creatives with specific project demand
- Use landing pages built for site visit conversion

## Build the conversion chain

- Send leads directly into CRM
- Trigger instant WhatsApp qualification
- Retarget warm leads with project proof and urgency

## Outcome

You reduce junk leads, improve sales team efficiency, and increase deal-ready conversations.
