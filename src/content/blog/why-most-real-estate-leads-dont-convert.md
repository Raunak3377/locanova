Lead volume is not revenue. Conversion happens when your sales process is engineered.

## Why leads drop

- Delayed follow-up after form submission
- No CRM visibility across team members
- No structured nurture for undecided buyers

## How to improve conversion

- Use a real estate CRM with clear stages
- Add WhatsApp follow-up workflows
- Run retargeting based on lead activity
- Track counselor response and closure rates

## Core takeaway

Connected ads, CRM, and follow-up systems create predictable project sales.
