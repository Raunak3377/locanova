## Step-by-Step Coaching Marketing in Delhi

### Step 1: Identify Your Delhi Target

Delhi coaching market segments:
- **JEE aspirants**: 11th-12th students, parents with ₹3-5L budget
- **NEET students**: Science stream, medical dreams, willing to pay ₹2-4L
- **CBSE board students**: 9th-12th, improving marks focus, ₹1-2L budget
- **Competitive exams**: SSC, Banking, Insurance, ₹50K-1L courses

Each needs different messaging and offer.

### Step 2: Run Multi-Channel Campaigns

**Campaign 1: Search Ads (Google)**
- Keywords: "Best JEE coaching Delhi", "NEET coaching near me", "11th science coaching"
- Monthly budget: ₹30,000
- Expected inquiries: 100-120
- Cost per inquiry: ₹250-300

**Campaign 2: Local Targeting (Meta)**
- Target: Parents age 35-55, income ₹50L+, Delhi neighborhoods (South Delhi, Dwarka, East Delhi)
- Ad creative: "35 students → IIT Delhi in 2 years" (specific, believable)
- Monthly budget: ₹40,000
- Expected inquiries: 130-150
- Cost per inquiry: ₹200-280

**Campaign 3: Retargeting**
- Show ads to website visitors, past inquiry holders
- Monthly budget: ₹15,000
- Expected conversions: 50-80
- Conversion rate: 20-30%

### Step 3: Send to Demo Class Landing Page

Your website shouldn't ask for admission directly. It should offer **free demo class**.

Why?
- Parents want to see teaching quality
- Demo class booking = purchase intent
- Converts at 35% (vs 5% for direct enrollment)

Landing page shows:
- Your batches (JEE, NEET, etc.)
- Success stats ("35 students → IIT")
- Teacher credentials
- CTA: "Book Free Demo Class"

### Step 4: Automate Parent Follow-Up

Parent inquires → They go cold if you don't respond in 2 hours.

Solution:
1. **Instant WhatsApp**: Sends demo class schedule, teacher intro, fees
2. **CRM tagging**: Hot (booked demo), Warm (interested), Cold (needs nurturing)
3. **Sales workflow**: Counselor calls hot leads immediately
4. **Drip campaigns**: Weekly tips, success stories, enrollment offers sent to warm leads

Result: **35% of inquiries convert to admissions** (vs 15% without automation)
